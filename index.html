About Spectachain

Blockchain In Reality

We introduce Specta Chain, a public blockchain protocol and smart contract platform that runs on PoSA (Proof of Stake Authority) consensus, which is a hybrid consensus mechanism between Proof of Stake and Proof of Authority. Specta Chain is designed to support WEB3, DApps, and Defi, and has features such as EVM compatibility, advanced security, scalability, Low memory consumption, deflation, Low cost, easy integration, and transaction speed ​​of up to 50.000 TPS.

Specta Chain features also support DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) to optimize decision-making within the blockchain network. With these advantages, Specta Chain can be an attractive choice for companies and individuals looking for a fast, secure, and cost-effective blockchain platform.
